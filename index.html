The 3 Real Problems With In-Person Training
❌ Problem #1 — You're Underpaid & Overworked

While some trainers break six figures, it comes with an immense sacrifice — trading dollars for hours 50+ hours a week. The income ceiling is real, and it's low.

❌ Problem #2 — You Can't Legally Coach on Nutrition

Personal trainers only address one small piece of the puzzle — workouts. Your clients are left figuring out nutrition alone, cycling through the same pattern: cut calories, lose 10 pounds, fall off, gain it back, blame you for the lack of results. It's not your fault — the model is broken.

❌ Problem #3 — You Only Get Paid When You're In Sessions

Cancellation? No pay. Day off? No pay. Vacation? No pay. And God forbid another 2020-style gym shutdown — you're left scrambling. Online training decouples your income from your presence.

🚫Years of "online experience" before you start
🚫A perfect brand or fancy website
✅ What You DO Need
✅Actually give a damn about client results — not just sessions
✅Ability to sell (you already do this every day as a trainer)
✅Willingness to show up online and connect — no different from the gym floor
✅Coachability and willingness to follow a proven system
✅Work ethic to build something real — not a get-rich-quick fantasy
The Bottom Line

Moving online = more freedom + more income for the coach. It also means a better, more complete experience for your clients. A trainer can only give 45 minutes a week. An online coach is in their client's corner 7 days a week.

How Do I Deliver Online Training?

Online coaching isn't just "Zoom workouts." When done right, it's a better experience than in-person — more comprehensive, more touchpoints, and more support.

In-Person Training Delivers
🏋️Workout guidance in sessions only
🥗Vague nutrition suggestions ("eat less sugar")
📱Text access between sessions (rarely used)
📅Accountability only on training days
Online Coaching Delivers
🤝1-1 Dedicated Coaching — available 7 days/week
📚Educational Materials for training AND nutrition via pre-recorded modules
🥦Customized Nutrition — individualized calories, macros & micros
📲App Access — like a personal trainer in their pocket at all times
📹Weekly Video Check-Ins — progress tracking + video adjustments
👥Private Community — accountability group for ongoing support

How to Price Your Online Coaching Offer

This is the biggest mental hurdle personal trainers face. Let's fix it right now — because undercharging will wreck your business, your confidence, and your quality of life.

You're Not Selling Workouts

You're selling a RESULT + YOU. Nobody walks into a gym wanting a personal training package. They want to lose 30 pounds. They want to feel good in their body again. That's what you're selling online — and that's worth a hell of a lot more than $25/hour.
